### The prayer modes (facts are not copyrightable — cite spellings to Idelsohn/survey)
- **Adonai Malach** ≈ major with ♭7 (Mixolydian core); does NOT repeat at octave; majestic. Harp: friendliest starter (set major, pre-flat the 7th).
- **Magein Avot** ≈ natural minor (1 2 ♭3 4 5 ♭6 ♭7), with a defining relative-major excursion; Sabbath peace. Harp: trivially diatonic.
- **Ahavah Rabbah / Freygish** = 1 ♭2 3 4 5 ♭6 ♭7 — the **augmented 2nd (♭2→3)** is its non-negotiable identity (= Phrygian-dominant = Arabic Hijaz = raga Bhairav). Harp: the aug-2nd is a lever-setup milestone.
- **Mi Sheberach / Ukrainian-Dorian** = 1 2 ♭3 ♯4 5 6 ♭7 (raised 4th); plaintive; mostly a connective/modulatory colour. Harp: advanced live lever-flip.
- Cross-cultural: Mixolydian≈Maqam Rast≈Raga Khamaj≈Adonai Malach; Aeolian≈Maqam Nahawand≈Magein Avot; Ahavah Rabbah≈Hijaz≈Bhairav.

## E. Science of sound & harp (for Shamayim Harp — cite honestly)
- **Music therapy / vibroacoustic therapy** — established peer-reviewed field (anxiety, pain, HRV, cortisol). Cochrane reviews.
- **Cymatics** — Ernst Chladni (1787) plate figures; Hans Jenny coined "cymatics" (1967). Vibration organises matter into patterns — real physics.
- **Frisson / musical chills** — Salimpoor et al., *Nature Neuroscience* (2011): dopamine release at peak musical moments.
- **Awe** — Keltner & Haidt (2003); Keltner, *Awe* (2023).
- **String / superstring / M-theory** — real theoretical physics (Green–Schwarz–Witten): matter as vibrating strings; 10 (superstring) / 11 (M-theory) dimensions; search for a single unified theory. *Present as physicists' leading framework, not proven fact.*
- **432 Hz** — HONESTY FLAG: claims of unique "healing/natural" superiority over 440 Hz are **not supported** by solid evidence; present as tradition/preference, not established science.
- **Archaeology** — Lyres of Ur (~2500 BCE), among the oldest known stringed instruments.
