# Jacob's Universe — Deep Resource Brief (web scan, 2026-06-25)
Curated, link-out-ready map of the richest theory sources on Jacob Collier's harmony, voicing, microtonality and production. Depth 1–5 (5 = dense/foundational). **All for learning-from + linking-out with attribution — never copied into the app.**

## 1. June Lee — the richest single theory vein
The primary source: Collier himself explaining his thinking, with on-screen transcriptions (filmed Feb 14 2017, Chicago).
- **Interview Pt 1** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DnBr070vcNE — negative harmony, circle-of-fifths lighten/darken, revived Ernst Levy's book. **D5** (we already have transcript: JC003)
- **Interview Pt 2** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b78NoobJNEo — microtonal voice leading, in-between notes, tuning. **D5** (JC004)
- **Interview Pt 3** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QujkcQMQFhg — tuning in "In the Bleak Midwinter," "Fascinating Rhythm," "Hideaway." **D5** (JC005)
- **"Why does this voicing feel so good?"** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmI1Bfxr_vA — voicing-by-voicing breakdown. **D4**
- **Harmony with June Ep.1: Collier's Half-Step Tricks** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MFD1nrx5iFM — June teaching Collier's chromatic moves. **D4**
