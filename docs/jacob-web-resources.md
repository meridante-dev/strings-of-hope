## 🟢/🔴 B. Foundational texts & the lineage behind his harmony
- **Ernst Levy — *A Theory of Harmony*** — 🟢 free PDF (archive.org, above) — the book Collier's circle revived; "polarity"/axis theory = direct ancestor of negative harmony. **D5.**
- **Steve Coleman — "Symmetrical Movement Concept"** — http://m-base.com/essays/symmetrical-movement-concept/ — 🟢 free on his site — Coleman *coined* "negative harmony"; builds on Levy (telluric/absolute, over/undertone). Primary written source. **D5.** (+ supplementary "Symmetric Questions" https://mbase.wordpress.com/2007/08/03/symmetric-questions/ )
- **Hugo Riemann — harmonic dualism / undertone series** — 🟢 *Vereinfachte Harmonielehre* (1893) public-domain on IMSLP https://imslp.org/wiki/Vereinfachte_Harmonielehre_(Riemann,_Hugo) ; context: MTO 11.2 Harrison review (open access) — the 19th-c. root of major=positive/minor=negative polarity Levy inherited. **D4.**
- **George Russell — *Lydian Chromatic Concept of Tonal Organization*** — 🔴 purchase-only https://georgerussell.com/lydian-chromatic-concept (ISBN 9780970373908). "Tonal gravity"/Lydian center. **Any free PDF is pirated — link the official page only.** **D4.**
- **Harry Partch — *Genesis of a Music*** — library-borrow on IA / purchase (ISBN 9780306801068) — foundational just-intonation/microtonality primer. **D4.**
- **Paul Hindemith — *The Craft of Musical Composition, Bk I*** — library-borrow on IA (ISBN 9780901938305) — overtone-series root theory behind reharmonisation logic. **D3.**
